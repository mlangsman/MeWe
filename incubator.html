Incubator

Our modern offices are just 2 minutes walk from Piccadilly Circus tube station in central London.

All members are free to use the open plan workspaces for a set number of hours per month depending on the membership type selected. Additional hours can be purchased on a pay as you go basis. All members are free to use the café facilities in the building throughout the week for informal meetings with other members or clients.

Members can also hire other rooms on an hourly or daily basis. The rooms vary from a small meeting room for 6 people to a large boardroom. We also have a cinema that can be hired for larger presentations or events.

Here's how it works

We’ll meet you for an initial in-depth one-to-one session so that together we can identify exactly what you need to propel you, your ideas and your creative business to the next level.
We then provide you with:

Mentoring

one-to-one coaching to guide your leadership development.

Consultancy

Expert advice provided by leading consultancies in key areas of business practice e.g. marketing, planning, finance, human resources.

Events

A programme of networking and leadership development events designed to inspire, inform and connect our membership including speakers, panel discussions, workshops, expert clinics. More...

Facilities

top end cinema, meeting, work and event spaces to present, collaborate and network. More...

Networks

you will be plugged into the MEWE network as well as other relevant external networks.

Launching a project or product?

We can provide the venue and help make sure the right people are there. Throughout the programme we will continue to work with you to closely monitor and respond to your changing needs as you, your business or idea develops. What you will get is a taylor made service to enable you to maximise your potential and opportunities.
