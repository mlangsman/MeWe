A new development house for ambitious and talented creative entrepreneurs

Our modern offices are just 2 minutes walk from Piccadilly Circus tube station in central London.

All members are free to use the open plan workspaces for a set number of hours per month depending on the membership type selected. Additional hours can be purchased on a pay as you go basis. All members are free to use the café facilities in the building throughout the week for informal meetings with other members or clients.

Members can also hire other rooms on an hourly or daily basis. The rooms vary from a small meeting room for 6 people to a large boardroom. We also have a cinema that can be hired for larger presentations or events.
